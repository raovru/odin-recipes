Tomato Pasta!

Description:

Don't we love a recipe we can finish completely in thirty or less minutes?

Here is a simple easy to follow recipe to cook veg tomato pasta. Let's get started!

Ingredients:

  • 6 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 2 tsp. kosher salt, divided
  • 6 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • Â¼ tsp. crushed red pepper
  • 1 lb. uncooked spaghetti
  • 2 lb. red and yellow cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Garlic finely chopped
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Steps:

  1. The first step is as follows:

    2. Heat remaining 4 tablespoons oil in same skillet over medium-high. Add garlic, and cook, stirring often, until aromatic, about 1 minute. Add tomatoes, crushed red pepper, and remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons salt. Cover and cook, stirring occasionally, until tomatoes are tender and release their juices, about 14 minutes.

  2. The next step is as follows:

    3. Heat remaining 4 tablespoons oil in same skillet over medium-high. Add garlic, and cook, stirring often, until aromatic, about 1 minute. Add tomatoes, crushed red pepper, and remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons salt. Cover and cook, stirring occasionally, until tomatoes are tender and release their juices, about 14 minutes.

  3. The next step is as follows:

    4. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high. Cook spaghetti until al dente. Drain spaghetti, reserving 1 cup pasta water.

  4. The final step is as follows:

    5. Add cooked spaghetti, Parmesan, and 1/2 cup reserved pasta water, tossing to coat. Cook, stirring occasionally, until sauce clings to pasta. Remove from heat. Garnish with fresh basil, if desired. Serve with additional Parmesan, if desired.
