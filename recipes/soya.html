Soya Chunks recipe!

Description:

We all love Gobi Manchurian, don't we?

But, we know how important protein is for our diet and that is where soya comes into the picture. Here is a simple recipe combining these two, so enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup Soya chunks
  • 2 tablespoon Corn flour white corn starch
  • 1/2 Capsicum
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 2 sprigs Spring onion
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Garlic finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon Soya sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Chilli sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Red chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder
  • 1 tablespoon Lemon juice or vinegar 1 tsp
  • 2 tablespoon Oil

Steps:

The first step is a standard boiling and cooking of the soya chunks.

The next step is to marinate:

  1. To the prepped soya chunks, add a tablespoon of corn flour, little salt, half the pepper powder, half the chilli powder and marinate.
  2. Keep aside for 15 mins.
  3. Cut capsicum length wise, chop onion, spring onion and gather all the required ingredients ready.

The final step is stove work:

  1. Heat a non stick pan with a teaspoon of oil and first toss the marinated soya chunks until the corn flour turns transparent.
  2. Heat the remaining oil and first fry ginger garlic for a minute without changing its color.
  3. Add onion, capsicum and fry till it becomes brown
  4. Now add sauces, pepper and add the tossed soya chunks and mix well
  5. Squirt the lemon or add vinegar and toss well. Lastly add the green part of spring onion
