Cabbage Soup!

Description:

A soothing recipe filled with fibres ad nutrients. Easy to drink, even when you are sick!

Ingredients:

  • Yellow Onions
  • 2 tsp. kosher salt, divided
  • 6 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • ¼ tsp. crushed red pepper
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Tomato sauce
  • Carrots
  • Bay leaf

Steps:

  1. The first step is as follows:

    2. Stir in consommé and tomato sauce; bring to a low boil. Stir in cabbage, carrots, rice, brown sugar, and bay leaf. Return to a boil; reduce heat to low, cover and simmer until rice is tender, about 25 minutes.

  2. The next step is as follows:

    3. Remove from heat; let stand, uncovered, 15 minutes. Discard bay leaf; stir in lemon and dill. Serve garnished with dill sprigs and lemon wedges.

  3. And finally:

    4. Remove from heat, and let stand, uncovered, 15 minutes. Remove and discard bay leaf; stir in lemon juice and chopped fresh dill.
